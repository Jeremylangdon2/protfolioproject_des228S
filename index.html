About me

My name is Jeremy, I am a UX designer and product strategist. I have been doing UX for almost 2 years now. I am a Visual communications student at SFSU. I enjoy desiging because I like putting things together. I think when desiging something there is an order to how things should be, and there is a balance. Doing user experience adds a whole nother level to designing. Now that you are designing something that has a more complex use, it takes more consideration. As a product strategist I look at the business aspect of a product, and I clairify what direction it needs to go in. This helps the rest of the company see where they are at, and where they need to be. It's fun, challenging, and takes a lot of thought. In my free time I like to play the piano, compose classical music, and hangout with my cat Bob. I live in Marin county, and enjoy living near the ocean. Most days you will either find me working or at school. One thing I'd like to do one day is become a classical composer. I could see myself as a conductor, or film composer. I think music can be very interesting and expressive. I don't use vocals ever, just instruments.
