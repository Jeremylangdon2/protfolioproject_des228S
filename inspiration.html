Inspirations

Michael Giacchino

Michael Giacchino is a film and game composer with a distinct sound. The music he creates is original, and has a unique tonal quality. It's very well done and in some cases almost perfect. Not only do I think he is a great composer, but I enjoy listening to the music he creates.

Michael’s IMDb

Coldplay

Coldplay is a band that I only recently came to enjoy. I like to listen to their live versions, especially the album "Live in Buenos Aires." Chris Martin, their lead singer and composer, creates everything by ear without reading sheet music, which inspires me as a pianist who also plays by ear.

Coldplay’s Website

Daft Punk

Daft Punk is an electronic music duo known for their originality and futuristic style. Their music blends electronic with other genres, and their concerts are like live DJ sets. Their ability to create unexpected and unique music is what inspires me.

Daft Punk Website
